### ⭐ Red Rock Canyon Star Parties - Las Vegas, NV (Nearby)
**Title:** "A Visitor From the Void: The Story of Interstellar Comet 3I/ATLAS"  
**Dates:** 
- February 21, 2026 (Saturday) 5:00 - 7:30 PM (Mars themed)
- March 21, 2026 (Saturday) 7:00 - 10:00 PM (Comet 3I/ATLAS themed)
**Location:** Red Rock Canyon Visitor Center, Las Vegas, NV (approx. 2 hours from California City)  
**Category:** Astronomy/Stargazing  
**Description:** Educational star parties featuring guest speakers, telescope viewing, and astronomy education. Part of effort to achieve Urban Night Sky Place designation. Features renowned astronomers like Matthias Schmitt from Cedar Breaks National Monument.  
**Source URL:** https://saveredrock.com/star-party/  
**Additional Info:** Over 1,200 people attended 2025 star parties. Bring warm clothes and red flashlight. Free event.
