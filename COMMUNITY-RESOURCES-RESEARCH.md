## 📊 Executive Summary

California City, founded in May 1958 and incorporated December 10, 1965, is the third largest incorporated city in land area in California and the eleventh largest in the United States. This comprehensive research provides detailed community resource information for residents and visitors.

## 📢 Recent News & Announcements

### Current City Developments (as of March 2026)
1. **Water Independence:** City actively working to become independent from AVEK Water Agency with 3 broken wells being fixed, water storage facility construction, and risk management plan development
2. **Infrastructure Projects:** Water storage facility and well repairs in progress
3. **Housing Development:** Desert Jade senior housing nearly full (vacant units fixed by volunteers), more senior housing planned with in-house housing manager to reduce consultant costs
4. **Staffing Improvements:** Full department director staffing achieved - Public Works Director, Planning Director, HR Director, Police Chief, and Fire Chief all hired
5. **Cost Savings:** Successfully moved away from consultants to in-house teams achieving multi-million dollar savings
6. **Recreation Projects:** New skatepark, splashpad, and clubhouse in bidding process with groundbreaking expected end of 2024 and completion Summer 2024
7. **Park Maintenance:** New reed cutter purchased for Central Park lake cattail management, City Hall grounds cleared and properly maintained
8. **District Elections:** Resolution adopted for transition from at-large to district-based elections
9. **Emergency Water Restrictions:** Currently in effect as of March 2026
